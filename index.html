Poke-Map Challenge

Game Instructions

Drag the map around to find all the Sprites.
Click the Sprite to launch the capture window.
Click the green circle when it overlaps with the red circle to capture the Sprite.
Once you capture the Sprite, click it to add it to your sprite pen. You can drag the Sprites in the pen to rearrange them.
